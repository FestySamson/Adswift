7.5 Fee Calculation Examples

Here are some examples of how fees are calculated:

Example 1: Cryptocurrency Trade

If you buy ₦100,000 worth of Bitcoin as a taker:

Fee = ₦100,000 × 2.0% = ₦2,000

Example 2: Airtime Purchase

If you purchase ₦5,000 airtime:

Fee = ₦5,000 × 2.0% = ₦100 (but minimum fee applies)
